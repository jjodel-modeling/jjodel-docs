Jjodel is a cloud-based platform — all you need is a browser and an account.

## Creating an Account

1. Navigate to [app.jjodel.io](https://app.jjodel.io)
2. Click **Sign Up**
3. Enter your email address and choose a password
4. Confirm your email by clicking the verification link sent to your inbox
5. Log in with your credentials

Registration is free and takes only a few seconds.

## Signing In

Once registered, visit [app.jjodel.io](https://app.jjodel.io) and enter your email and password. You will be redirected to your personal **Dashboard**.

## Password Recovery

If you forget your password:

1. Click **Forgot Password** on the sign-in page
2. Enter the email address associated with your account
3. Follow the instructions in the recovery email to set a new password

## Browser Support

Jjodel is optimized for all modern, standards-compliant browsers. For the best experience, we recommend:

- **Google Chrome** (latest)
- **Microsoft Edge** (latest)

Other Chromium-based browsers are generally supported, though performance may vary slightly.

:::note
Jjodel requires JavaScript to be enabled. If you encounter issues, make sure your browser is up to date and that no extensions are blocking scripts on the page.
:::
