## Creating an Account

1. Go to [app.jjodel.io](https://app.jjodel.io)
2. Click **Register** (or the "Register" link at the bottom of the Login form)
3. Fill in the registration form:
   - **First Name** and **Last Name** (required)
   - **Nickname** (optional)
   - **Affiliation** (your university or organization, e.g., `univaq`)
   - **Country** (select from the dropdown)
   - **Email** (use your institutional email if available)
   - **Password** (minimum 8 characters) and **Confirm Password**
4. Optionally, subscribe to the Jjodel newsletter
5. Click **Create**
6. Confirm your email by clicking the verification link sent to your inbox

Registration is free and takes only a few seconds.

![Registration form](./images/registration-form.png)

## Signing In

Once registered, visit [app.jjodel.io](https://app.jjodel.io) and enter your email and password. Click **Login** to access your personal Dashboard.

![Login form](./images/login-form.png)

## Password Recovery

If you forget your password:

1. Click **Forgot your password?** on the Login page
2. Enter the email address associated with your account
3. Click **Retrieve**
4. Follow the instructions in the recovery email to set a new password

![Password recovery form](./images/password-recovery-form.png)

## Browser Support

Jjodel runs in all modern, standards-compliant browsers. For the best experience, use the latest version of Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge.

:::note
Jjodel requires JavaScript to be enabled. If you encounter issues, make sure your browser is up to date and that no extensions are blocking scripts on the page.
:::
